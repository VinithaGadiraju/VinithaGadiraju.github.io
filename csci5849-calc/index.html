About this app

Welcome!
You will be using a 3-key switch interface to carry out arithmetic expressions using this calculator. You will start with either your elbow or right hand to perform the expressions. When you are done, you will switch and use the other body part to complete the same expressions again.
To navigate the app, you may use the following keys: Press "h" to move through the items in a row. Press "y" to move through the items in a column. Press "b" to select a button once you have scrolled to it using the keys listed above.
Use the calculator to perform the following arithmetic expressions:
3 x 7
12 - 40
89 + 7
51 / 6
343 - 591
482 x 7
Be sure to clear the form between each expression!

Test Results

4 participants took part in the study. All participants were female, grad students, and in their early 20s. All participants did both the elbow and hand trials. 2 participants did the elbow trial first and then the hand trial. 2 participants did the right hand trial first and then the elbow trial.
All participants spent longer using their elbow than they did using their right hand (see time table below). They also expressed more frustration at using the calculator during the elbow trials (see quotes below).
